02 / 09
The Opportunity

Loyalty programmes
have an engagement problem

  • Customers sign up, earn points, and forget about them
  • Static point systems create no emotional connection to the brand
  • Paper-based rewards are costly, wasteful, and difficult to track
  • Competitors are moving toward interactive, digital-first experiences

Our platform delivers points well. It doesn't yet deliver a reason to care. Gamification closes that gap.

03 / 09
Market Research

This is already proven at scale

🍔
McDonald's Monopoly
Collect & Win
Peel-to-reveal stickers tied to purchases. Collect property sets to win prizes. Ran for decades across dozens of markets — one of the most successful loyalty mechanics ever built.
🛒
Lidl Plus App
Digital Scratch Cards
Every shop unlocks a digital scratch card in the app. A direct driver of app downloads and repeat visits. Simple mechanic, massive adoption — identical to our proposed model.
☕
Starbucks Rewards
Challenges + Bonus Stars
Gamified star challenges, double-star days, and limited-time games inside the app. One of the most engaged loyalty programmes in the world — gamification is central to it.
🛍️
Spin-to-Win (E-commerce)
Wheel + Email Capture
Used by thousands of Shopify stores via tools like Privy and Wheelio. Email captured in exchange for a spin. The exact mechanic we are proposing — already a proven conversion driver.
🎰
7-Eleven App
In-App Mini Games
Games rewarding free Slurpees and discounts drove a major surge in app downloads and daily active users. Gamification became the primary acquisition tool for their digital programme.
🥤
Coca-Cola "Under the Cap"
Code Entry + Prize Draw
Enter codes from bottle caps for prize entries. Billions of interactions over the programme's lifetime. Proved that a simple reward mechanic tied to a physical action drives massive repeat engagement.
Anthic is not inventing gamified loyalty — it is bringing these proven mechanics natively into a B2B platform, which is the gap the market is missing.
04 / 09
The Concept

Earn a token.
Play a game.
Win more.

A hybrid skill-and-chance reward layer built directly into the existing Anthic platform — configurable per merchant, seamlessly digital.

Always guaranteed

Every qualifying action earns 1 token automatically. No one walks away empty handed.

Bonus game

Customers are then invited to play for additional tokens — blending skill and chance for a fair, engaging experience.

Save paper. Earn rewards.

Customers ditch the paper receipt and go fully digital — earning tokens as they do it.

08 / 09
Legal & IP

The hybrid model is
deliberate, not accidental

🎰
Pure Chance
❌ Legal Exposure
Classified as gambling in most jurisdictions. Not viable as a standalone mechanic.
🧠
Pure Skill
⚠️ Economic Risk
Outcomes become unpredictable. Hard to control point costs per player at scale.
🎰 + 🧠
Our Hybrid Model
Guaranteed minimum token removes the gambling classification risk
Skill element provides legal cover across our core markets
Point economy stays predictable and controllable for merchants
Pure Chance ▲ Sweet Spot Pure Skill
We are here

Patent landscape research conducted — a clear opportunity exists to establish a defensible position as development progresses.
